At Bihar Chhatra Sansad, we provide a meaningful platform for youth to engage in a positive political discussion, foster critical thinking, and voice their opinions. Confluence of Potential: This summit bridges generations and ideologies, sparking inspiration, ideas, and visionary leadership. Join the Movement: Experience the power of voices uniting for a stronger and a more egalitarian, entrepreneurial Bihar.
